## Selected Projects

**Sentinel Pro: Real-Time Fraud and Compliance Monitor**
Live streaming pipeline processing 100+ transactions per second with sub-500ms latency. Hybrid anomaly detection engine combining regulatory rules with Isolation Forest. Identified simulated $5M+ anomalies in a high-frequency banking environment.

**Agentic AI Credit Risk Scoring Engine**
Basel III compliant credit scorecard using Weight of Evidence and Logistic Regression, integrated with a Llama-3 agent for natural language loan decision explanations. Bridges black-box modeling with audit-ready transparency.

**Dual-Signal Trading Bot**
Stacked BiLSTM neural network merging structured OHLCV market data with NLP-based news sentiment (NLTK/VADER) to generate alpha signals and identify divergence between sentiment and T+1 price movements.

**Markets Quantitative Analysis (Citi Simulation)**
Applied Black-Scholes and Cost of Carry models for options and futures pricing. Ran 1,000+ Monte Carlo scenarios to calculate portfolio VaR and designed delta-hedging strategies.

**MSc Dissertation: Personalised Explainable AI (XAI)**
Dropout risk prediction framework for educators using XGBoost with SHAP and LIME interpretability layers, and K-Means student persona segmentation. AUC of 0.92. Identified assignment delays as a top risk factor for 60% of at-risk students.

**Heart Disease Prediction**
XGBoost classification pipeline with 5-fold cross-validation, SHAP explainability, and Optuna hyperparameter tuning. AUC of 0.92.

**NLP: Airline Review Sentiment Analysis**
Compared TF-IDF + SVM (87.8% accuracy) against a Stacked BiLSTM with custom Word2Vec embeddings (85.5% accuracy) for predicting airline recommendations from customer reviews.

**Big Data: Spark Optimisation on GCP**
Benchmarked JPEG vs TFRecord I/O formats and applied Bayesian optimisation principles for cluster sizing on Google Cloud Dataproc. TFRecord outperformed across throughput metrics.
